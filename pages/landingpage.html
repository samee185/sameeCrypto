About SAMEE XVIII-V & CO Investment Group.

SAMEE XVIII-V & CO Investment Group is an Africa-centric integrated alternative payment and e-commerce company that facilitates the easy use of digital currencies like Bitcoin, Ethereum and other digital assets for everyday transactions. SAMEE XVIII-V & CO Investment Group established her first branch in Ibadan, Oyo state on May 3rd, 2023, with a small office set up to serve less than 80 clients.

It was founded by Comrade, Iyanda Samuel Oluwatosin, who wanted to create a safe & secure platform for users seeking to exchange their cryptocurrencies for cash after he had a bad experience trading his. This dream to create a trusted ecosystem for users like him led to the birth of SAMEE XVIII-V & CO Investment. For Samee Crypto, building user-centric products, as well as focusing on practical solutions has been indispensable to her market growth.
